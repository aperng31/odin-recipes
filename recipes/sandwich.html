Egg and Bacon Sandwich

Hearty and filling sandwich for breakfast

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 strips of bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of maple syrup

Steps

  1. Dip slices of bread in beaten eggs and place in frying pan at medium heat until browned, then set aside
  2. Cook bacon in a skillet until brown, the drain and set aside. Reserve 1 tablespoon of bacon grease
  3. Fry eggs and place on top of 2 slices of toast
  4. Top eggs with bacon and remaining 2 slices of toast, then finish with maple syrup

Time to eat!
