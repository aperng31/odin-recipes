Watermelon Slushie

Make a Tasty Watermelon Smoothie for a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of watermelon
  • 10 ice cubes
  • 1/3 cup of lime juice
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Place watermelon and ice in blender
  2. Place lime juice, sugar, and salt in blender
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Add more lime juice, sugar, and salt as desired

And that's it!
